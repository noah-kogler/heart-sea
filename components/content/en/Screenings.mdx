### World premiere

The world premiere of “Throw your heart into the sea” will take place at the

<CrossingEuropeLink />

Film-Festival between **29th of April and 4th of May 2025**.

<br />

Tickets will be available from 22nd of April 2025. Also, the exact time of the screening will be revealed by then.

Further details about the premiere can be found in the article about
[Feature film world premieres in the Competition Local Artists](https://www.crossingeurope.at/news/langfilm-weltpremieren_in_der_competition_local_artists)
on the festival website.
